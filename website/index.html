Visual Effects otherwise known as VFX can be described as the art of combining different styles of visual storyteling, different art styles, and different components to create one cohesive art piece to tell a story.

Even within vfx despite what you may think there are more types of work than just art. For example what sims used to be very simple and have now become more complex not just due to better technology but also Creative mathematicians

the forces shaping visual effects
phase one
early production
concept artist

Concept Artists work with directors to create different ideas for scenes, objects, and characters.

PRE VIS ARTIST

Pre Visualization artists work with the director and 3d modelers to create rough 3d visualizations of shots

phase two
main production
3D MODELER

3D Modelers job is to create models of people, animals, and objects that can later be animated.

ANIMATOR

Animators Take A rigged 3D model and animate it either using keyframe animation or MoCAP Data

ENVIRONMENTAL ARTIST

Environmental Artists Use Procedural tools with hand refinement to create scenes that will be seen in final renders

TEXTURE ARTIST

Texture Artist Create the final Textures for 3D models Using A combination of photos, hand drawn art, and procedural tools

phase three
late production
SPECIAL EFFECTS

Special Effect Artist use many tools generally simulations to create realistic effects like fire or smoke or other Effects

COMPOSITING ARTIST

Compositing artist use and combine all of the aspects from different artist and turn them into a finished film

examples
Some examples of VFX
Arcane Season Two

Arcane season one and two are great examples that visual effects can be more than just combining cgi and live action shots as the creators of arcane use a combination of both 3d and 2d animation styles.

AVATAR: THE WAY OF WATER

James Cameron's Avatar series is a great example of creating films that would not have been possible without the genius of the minds behind it.

STAR WARS: A NEW HOPE

Star Wars: A New Hope is a great example of how impactful visual effects was to earlier age of filmmaking by showing science fiction in a non drawn art style which was unheard of at the time.

INTERSTELLAR

Interstellar is a great example of being able to use visual effects for things other than just entertainment, while interstellar is still a entertainment film the simulations used to make the blackhole and wormholes where so accurate that they discovered some things not previously before known.

Christopher Nolan's Oppenheimer is often criticized for not using cgi to create a realist nuclear explosion which they instead opted for a small scale tnt explosion that wasn't even big enough to get a similar look

The Battle Of the Five armies is often criticized for using cgi to create the facial features of some of the orcs as fans preferred the more rough less polished look that the practical makeup approach had.
